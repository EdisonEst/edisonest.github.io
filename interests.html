My Interests and Hobbies


Aside from academics, I am passionate about various activities that keep me engaged and entertained. Below are a few of my favorite hobbies:

Baseball

Baseball has always been a passion of mine. I play in my free time and enjoy watching games whenever I can. The teamwork, strategy, and excitement of the game keep me coming back.

Music

Music is another big part of my life. I listen to various genres and find that it enhances my productivity and mood. Whether I'm working out, studying, or relaxing, music is always there to set the tone.

Video Games

Video games have been a major part of my life for many years. I enjoy playing both solo and multiplayer games across various platforms. Whether it's RPGs, action games, or competitive esports, gaming provides an exciting escape.

Babysitting

Babysitting has taught me valuable skills like patience, responsibility, and creativity. I enjoy helping young children engage in fun and educational activities, ensuring they have a great time and stay safe.
